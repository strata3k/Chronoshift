About Us

Chronoshift Expeditions offers real person to person assistance and 3D assistance to suit the traveler’s needs. The guides have plenty of experience traveling and hunting in the future. All of the guides are fit to provide the best experience for the expedition they so choose.

Petra Gurin

Petra Gurin is one of the first developed AI’s to help our travelers. She was made in 2020 and was developed to provide the basic assistance for all travelers. Petra Gurin is only available for sightseeing.

Sana Minatozaki

Sana was a member of the Korean pop girl group TWICE. She joined Chronoshift because of her extensive knowledge on animals and experience with safaris. She’s been a part of Chronoshift for six years and has been working as a guide for five. Sana is able to help the traveler with hunting their game and with sightseeing.

Momo Hirai

Momo is from the former Korean pop girl group TWICE. She joined with Sana because of the group disabandoning. Momo serves as a sightseeing guide and hunter as well.

Amelia Watson

Amelia Watson is from Hololive EN. She is a time traveling detective and with a partnership with Hololive, Chronoshift is able to bring her to the past! She is a 3D model of course, but can help out the travelers with problems they come across. Amelia Watson is only available for sightseeing.
